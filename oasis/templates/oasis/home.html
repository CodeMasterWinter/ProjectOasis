Our Values

Belong

We believe our utmost desire is to get the people of God to belong irrespective of where and who they are, what class and or status they bare.

Believe

That when the people of God know and understand the vision and mission, will be willing to fulfil the mandate given to the local church.

Behave

We believe that when people embrace the vision and mission they will be taught how to behave and follow the doctrine of Christ.
